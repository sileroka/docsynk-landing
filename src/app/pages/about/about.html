Our Story

DocSynk was born from frustration. Our founders spent years in freight forwarding and customs brokerage, watching teams waste hours on paperwork that should take minutes. In 2023, we decided enough was enough.

We assembled a team of logistics veterans and AI engineers with one goal: make international shipping documentation as easy as sending an email. Today, we process over 100,000 documents monthly for companies across six continents.

But we're just getting started. Every day, we're building new features, expanding to new countries, and helping more companies focus on growing their business instead of fighting paperwork.
